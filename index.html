AI 101 for Patients

Your guide to using AI tools like ChatGPT safely and effectively with your health information. No technical background needed.

AI can now connect to your medical records. Learn how to use these powerful tools to better understand your health—while knowing when to trust AI and when to call your doctor.

Who This Guide Is For

  • Anyone who uses or wants to use AI tools for health questions
  • Patients managing chronic conditions who want AI to help
  • Caregivers helping family members navigate healthcare
  • Anyone curious about ChatGPT Health and similar tools

What This Guide Is Not

This is not medical advice. AI tools are not a replacement for your doctor. This guide helps you use AI as one tool among many—while knowing its limits and when to seek real medical care.
